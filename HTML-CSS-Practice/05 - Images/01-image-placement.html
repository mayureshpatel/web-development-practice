Where to Place Images in Your Code

Placing an Image Before a Paragraph

These colognes are some of the best ones to come out in 2016. I bought these from the Tanger Outlet at a store
called Perfumania. I thought the store itself was really small and didn't have much of a selection but they were
organized which made the shopping experience great. I found what I wanted in just a few minutes and then shopped
the non-brand-name colognes and found one that I really liked. It was cheaper than the brand-name ones to begin
with and they ended up taking off another 25% from its price because I also bought 2 name-brand ones.
